# Schizophrenia GWAS Analysis — Claude Context
## Project Overview
This project analyzes PGC3 schizophrenia GWAS summary statistics 
to identify genome-wide significant loci and perform pathway 
enrichment analysis. The goal is to connect genetic variants to 
biological pathways disrupted in schizophrenia.

## Biological Question
Which biological pathways and processes are implicated in 
schizophrenia based on genome-wide association evidence?

## Data
- PGC3_SCZ_wave3.primary.autosome.public.v3.tsv.gz
- Source: Psychiatric Genomics Consortium 2022 Nature paper
- ~8 million SNPs across autosomes
